Typhoon knowledge base – modern fighter aircraft (public info, simplified)

F-16 Fighting Falcon
- American single-engine multirole fighter.
- Known for agility, huge export base, and continuous upgrades.

F-15EX Eagle II
- Modernised F-15 variant focused on payload, range, and updated avionics.
- Not stealth; designed for large weapons loads and high performance.

Dassault Rafale
- French multirole fighter with strong sensor integration and electronic warfare emphasis.
- Naval-capable versions support carrier operations.

Saab JAS 39 Gripen
- Swedish lightweight multirole fighter designed for lower operating costs.
- Often described as optimised for dispersed operations and quick turnaround.

Sukhoi Su-35
- Russian heavy multirole fighter known for manoeuvrability and thrust vectoring.
- Specific performance figures vary by source and configuration.

Chengdu J-20
- Chinese fifth-generation fighter with a focus on low observability and long-range roles.
- Many details are not fully public; treat specifics cautiously.

F-35 Lightning II
- Fifth-generation stealth multirole fighter with strong sensor fusion and networking.
- Multiple variants exist for different services.

General notes
- “Best fighter” depends on mission, training, support, and constraints.
- Exact radar and EW figures are often not public.
- If asked for precise numbers, respond with caveats.
